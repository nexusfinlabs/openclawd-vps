# OpenClaw Email Style Guide

> **Instrucciones para la IA (System Prompting):**
> A continuación se presentan ejemplos reales del estilo corporativo del usuario (Alberto Lobo). Tu objetivo estricto es imitar este tono profesional, directo, la longitud de los párrafos, las fórmulas de saludo (ej. "Dear Mr. X," o "Hola [Nombre],") y las despedidas.
> El contexto proporcionado en el prompt del usuario ("context", "objetivo") te indicará qué estilo o plantilla base debes tomar como referencia y adaptar a la empresa objetivo.
> **Regla de oro:** La primera línea de tu respuesta DEBE ser siempre el Asunto, prefijado por "Subject: " o "Asunto: ".

---

## Estilo 1: M&A / Transacciones (Contexto Alto, Venta de Activo)
**Cuándo usar:** Cuando el contexto indique presentar una oportunidad de M&A, oportunidad de adquisición (pre-emptive), mandato de venta, o contacto con inversores/boutiques.

**Asunto:**
`Pre-emptive Acquisition Opportunity | High-Quality Asset in your sector`

**Cuerpo:**
Dear [Nombre],

Through my sector network, I became aware of a high-quality acquisition opportunity before it comes to market. The information came directly through long-standing relationships, which is why I'm reaching out selectively.

The target is a highly synergistic asset operating in the same specific vertical as [Empresa Destino]. [Aquí la IA debe insertar unas 2-3 líneas de contexto explicando por qué encaja exactamente con la empresa].

The sellers would consider a pre-emptive transaction with the right buyer, but only at the appropriate EV/EBITDA multiple. Otherwise, they are prepared to launch a formal market process, which would likely become competitive.

Given your group’s strategy, we believe this could be a strong fit. Let me know if you’d like to review a brief overview and discuss a potential pre-emptive approach.

Best Regards,
Alberto Lobo
M&A Tech Advisor
https://www.linkedin.com/in/ajleblob/

## Estilo 2: Desarrollo de Negocio / Propuesta de Colaboración Freelance
**Cuándo usar:** Cuando el contexto indique buscar alianzas estratégicas, colaborar en desarrollo de negocio, o proponer servicios como Contractor/Freelance.

**Asunto:**
`Business Development & Strategic Partnerships | Freelance Contractor Proposal`

**Cuerpo:**
Dear Mr. [Apellido],

My name is Alberto Lobo, Tech Entrepreneur and Business Development Advisor with 18+ years delivering complex engineering and digital transformation projects across highly regulated and technology-driven industries.

I am reaching out with a proposal to collaborate with [Empresa Destino] as a Freelance Business Development Contractor.

[Empresa Destino]'s product range is technically compelling and commercially underexploited in several high-growth markets. DC rectifiers, Pulse Reverse power supplies, E-coating rectifiers and the new Container and Skid Models for hydrogen production and electrolysis sit at the intersection of industrial electrochemistry and the global energy transition. That is a strong commercial story that deserves aggressive market development.

I bring a network of industrial, manufacturing and engineering contacts across Southern Europe, Latam and internationally, combined with the ability to identify and close strategic partnerships with companies in adjacent sectors such as PCB manufacturing, anodizing, water treatment, hydrogen production and surface finishing.

Concretely, I can contribute in the following areas:

Identifying and approaching potential distributors, system integrators and OEM partners in markets where [Empresa Destino] has limited presence. Developing co-selling arrangements with complementary technology providers in the electrochemical and Power-to-X space. Supporting international business development on a results-oriented Freelance Contractor basis.

I would welcome the opportunity to have an initial conversation to explore whether there is a fit.

Best Regards,
Alberto Lobo
Tech Entrepreneur & Business Development Advisor
https://www.linkedin.com/in/ajleblob/
